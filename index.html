Our Brand Story

Welcome to the Kin Brand Guidelines. This document outlines the essential elements of the Kin brand, ensuring consistency and clarity in all our communications. Kin is an e-commerce retailer dedicated to providing elegant fashion accessories, including watches, sunglasses, and wallets, for your everyday style in Bangladesh.

Kin represents the connection between our customers and the meticulously crafted fashion items we offer. We believe in bringing elegance, precision, and quality directly to your doorstep, enriching your daily style with accessories that are both beautiful and functional. Our brand embodies sophistication, quality, and modern fashion sensibilities.

Logo

Primary Logo

The Kin logo is the cornerstone of our visual identity. It features a stylized "Kin" wordmark with a shopping bag icon integrated into the 'K', symbolizing our e-commerce nature and the fashion items we deliver.

Clear Space

Always maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the 'i' in "Kin". No other graphic elements should infringe upon this space.

Minimum Size

To ensure legibility, the logo should not be reproduced smaller than 1 inch (or 72 pixels) in width for print, and 32px height for digital.

Imagery

Imagery should reflect the elegance, sophistication, and quality of our fashion accessories (watches, sunglasses, wallets). The style is clean, minimalist, and uses high-quality product photography with natural lighting and simple backgrounds. Lifestyle shots should convey style, precision, and everyday elegance.

Tone of Voice

Our tone of voice is elegant, sophisticated, and inviting, reflecting the quality and style of our fashion accessories and our commitment to our customers.

Elegant & Sophisticated

Reflects the premium quality and refined style of our fashion items. Language is polished and discerning, emphasizing craftsmanship and design.

Informative & Precise

Provides clear and concise information about our products' features, materials, and benefits. Highlights the unique aspects of each watch, pair of sunglasses, or wallet.

Inviting & Inspirational

Creates a welcoming and trustworthy experience, inspiring customers to elevate their everyday style. Language is warm, respectful, and customer-focused, encouraging exploration of our collections.

Applications

Website & E-commerce

The website uses a clean, intuitive layout, designed to showcase our fashion accessories prominently. Primary colors dominate, with Accent Gold for key calls-to-action. Inter is used for all primary text for maximum readability, ensuring a seamless shopping experience for watches, sunglasses, and wallets.

Social Media

Visuals are consistent and high-quality, focusing on lifestyle and fashion trends featuring our watches, sunglasses, and wallets. The logo is used subtly. The tone of voice is engaging and informative, inspiring style and elegance. Use hashtags like #KinBangladeshFashion, #EverydayElegance, and #KinAccessories.

Packaging

Packaging design is minimalist and elegant, using premium, eco-friendly materials suitable for luxury fashion accessories. It features the Kin Navy Blue and Black, with the logo displayed prominently, enhancing the unboxing experience for watches, sunglasses, and wallets.
